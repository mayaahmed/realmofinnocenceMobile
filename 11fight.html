Chapter 11

The Big Brawl

"Can I borrow some matchsticks?" asked Poonam. Poonam was Tara's neighbor who was a lot older than her.

"Mother is not home. Come back later for it" said Tara.

"Give it to me now. I cannot wait for it till your mother's return. I need it now" shouted Poonam.

Tara's siblings came out to see who was shouting. They did not like Poonam's attitude at all. Without saying a word to each other, the children had decided Poonam is not getting any match sticks from them. Poonam started to use swear words. The children and Poonam began a war of words. Poonam decided to go home. But she was fuming. She came out to the balcony and started yelling at them.

Thus began the balcony fight. It became really loud. People walking on the roads below had stopped to watch. Poonam was no match. The children really had a talent for hurling insults. Poonam was called elephant a lot. Moreover, while Poonam was fuming, the children were really having a great time. Tara's team was clearly winning this fight.

The fight came to a sudden halt when Tara's mother returned. The kids got a good thrashing. She had heard the brawl from quite a distance. She was shocked to see her own children making all the noise. Tara's mother was red with anger and embarrassment.

Poonam was nowhere to be seen for the next few days. She did not take the opportunity to gloat. Perhaps, she was afraid of Tara's mother too. The fight was never mentioned again.
