Chapter 10

Kicked Out Of Home

Tara was in tears. She simply could not write the alphabet C. She could write every other alphabet except C. Her mother was losing patience. Tara was really trying hard.

"Get out of the house. I do not want to raise a daughter who cannot write C" declared Tara's mother. She was shown the door.

Tara sat outside the door crying. A neighbor came and scolded Tara's mother.

"How can you behave like this to a child? If you do not want her, I am taking her to my house" the neighbor said.

"She just cannot write C. I don't know what to do. I am at my wits end" said Tara's mother.

"Come with me child. Your mother is no good. You can stay in my house" said the neighbor.

The neighbor took Tara to her house. Tara went and sat quietly in a corner. She looked around at her surroundings. So this was going to be her new house. Did she like it? Not so much. The neighbor lady was kind. That was a good thing. Tara decided to adjust. The lady offered her food but Tara had none. Tara watched the lady and her kids eating lunch. This was her new family.

After a good twenty minutes Tara's brother came and said "Mother is calling Tara home".

"Do you want to go? You can stay here with us." said the lady.

"Thank you. But I think I will go home" said Tara.

"Are you sure?" asked the lady.

"Yes. I am sure" said Tara.

Tara was really happy to get back home. Few days later she even learned how to write C.
