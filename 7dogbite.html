Chapter 7

The Dog Bite

Mothi was a friendly dog. Children loved playing with him.

One day someone gave Mothi a big, fat, and juicy bone. Geeta decided to pet Mothi when Mothi was chewing on the bone. Mothi growled for a few minutes before biting the cheek of Geeta. Geeta ran home bleeding and crying.

Soon a crowd gathered around Mothi's owner. Mothi was declared a dangerous dog. He had to be put to sleep. The owner agreed with a heavy heart. Mothi was taken far away from home and left at an animal shelter.

All the children including Geeta was sad. This was disproportionate punishment for Mothi. He was going to pay with his life for a bite.

After a few days, Mothi came back looking tired, hungry, and disheveled. He had somehow found his way back home.

"Dogs are incredible animals. How they can find their way back home like this is a mystery" said Mothi's owner.

All was forgiven. Everyone was happy to see Mothi again.

"If we do not disturb a dog while eating, we have a friend for life" said Tara happily.
