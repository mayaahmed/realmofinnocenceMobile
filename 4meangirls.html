Chapter 4

Mean Girls

Tara, Lata, Geeta, and some other girls would walk together to music class. Lata liked to play a mean trick on Geeta time and again. She would ask all the girls to leave for class without waiting for Geeta. And every time Geeta arrived late to class, Lata would secretly snigger away. Tara never liked this trick but her protests were ignored. And one day, Tara found herself arriving late to class to meet a sniggering Lata.

"Lata and her friends did not wait for me today. They are playing the trick they play on Geeta, regularly, on me too" complained Tara to her mother.

"Is that so? Do not wait for them from tomorrow on wards" declared Tara's mother.

So the next class Tara reached way before any of our friends. When Lata saw Tara she was surprised.

"Why did you not wait for us?" asked Lata.

"Last time you were not keen on waiting for me. I thought you may not wait for me today also. So I came on my own" said Tara.

"Last time was a genuine mistake. It will never happen again. Please wait for us. Let us walk to class together" pleaded Lata.

"Okay" said Tara.

Lata kept her promise. She never played silly tricks on Tara again.
