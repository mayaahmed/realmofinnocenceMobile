Chapter 8

The Fablers

One day, while visiting a park, Tara and her brother saw a crow's nest with birdlings. They had never seen a bird's nest before. They were full of questions. They were thrilled by the bird's nest.

A couple of days later Tara said " When I went to play today, I saw a peacock's nest. The peacock gave me a chocolate. It was very tasty."

"How come you did not show it to me?" asked her brother.

"The peacock said I should not talk about his secret nest to anyone. Only I am allowed to visit. The peacock trusts me." said Tara.

"That is okay. I found an eagle's nest the other day. He gave me a delicious cake. He also gave me some toys." said Tara's brother.

"My peacock gave me toys too. In fact he gave me a big doll. I left it near his nest. I will go back and play with it later" said Tara.

For days afterwards, Tara and her brother would talk about all the animal friends they had found in secret. These included elephants, lions, rabbits, crocodiles, and so on. One day Tara decided that she had taken the game too far.

"I was just making up stories of those animal friends. I do not have any" said Tara.

"Me too" said her brother. And that was the end of story telling for the two of them for a while.
